Header

The header works as a navigation-bar for your website. You can modify it by adding is-fixed or is-sticky to either make it fixed to the top, or to be a sticky-positioned item.

Within a header, a container or a flex-container should hold your content. You can place a header-items, and a header-menu as children to the container. You can then add header-items and header-dropdowns to the header-menu.

A header-menu and its contents is visible on desktops, but hidden on mobiles and tablets by default, but you can easily show it as a dropdown-menu by adding the class is-active. It can contain both header-items and header-dropdowns. (Archipelago does not include any javascript.)

You can change the apperance of your header by adding is-# where # is one of the theme-colors.
