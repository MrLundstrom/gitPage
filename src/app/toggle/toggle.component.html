Toggle

The toggle componenet lets you show and hide content easily depending on the state of the toggle and what device you are using. There are 3 different toggles which behave differently depending on the viewport-size. You can add a toggle class to most type of elements.

the regular 'toggle' is hidden on all devices, unless you add 'is-active' to the class.

the toggle 'is-tablet' is hidden on mobile, unless you add 'is-active' to the class.

the toggle 'is-desktop' is hidden on mobile and tablet, unless you add 'is-active' to the class.
