Headers

Headers (<h1> - <h6>) use the font-family 'Quicksand, sans-serif’ and have font-sizes from 1rem to 3rem, together with a font-weight of 500.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs (<p>) have a font-size of 1rem , 1.5 in line-spacing and uses the font-family 'Quicksand, sans-serif’ with 300 in font-weight.

If you would like to use this font-family copy the link below into your website's <head>

If you use one of the color classes, the color of the text is automatically set, but you can also add a text color by adding the text-color classes.

text-main, text-danger, text-link etc.

There is also a possibility to create quotes

Lastly, you can align your text to the left, center and right.
