Splash

a splash is a large color/image element that is meant to catch the users view instantly. It can contain most types of elements, and comes in four height (is-25/50/75/100) that takes up that amount of the page - your header-height.You should a container/flex-container inside of it if you wish to add something. If you dont set a size, it scales with your content. It is always 100% width. The content inside is centered vertically.
